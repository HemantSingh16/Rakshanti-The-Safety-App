SOCIAL POLICE

1.Who is Social Police?

The volunteer users will register themselves as Social police. Social police will be helping the surrounding community and help create a better place to live.

2.Why the concept of Social Police?

Safety is Everyone's responsibility.Thinking of police man,a picture of protector, helper comes into our mind.So why not bring out a police soul from a common man!

3.Responsibilty of Social Police?

You need to share your location in the application.So when any one near you is feeling unsafe, you will getting the notification about the victim's location and you will need to help the victim. The Social Police has choice to approve for help or decline for the help . Follow the saftey guidelines and by standard guide,so that we ensure your saftey and you need to ensure victim's safety.
